Environmental Studies - Grade 4

Environmental Studies is a multidisciplinary field that explores the relationship between humans and the environment. It focuses on understanding environmental issues, conservation, and sustainable practices. Through research and education, Environmental Studies aims to promote ecological awareness and foster responsible actions to protect the planet for future generations.
